Please follow the below steps to get the Facebook Access Token
Step 1:   Visit - Facebook API Explorer.
Step 2:   Click on 'Get Token' -> 'Get User Access Token' and select all the checkboxes/fields in the new window.

NOTE:
You may have to login first through your facebook account to get the 'Access Token'
Step 3:   Click on 'Get Access Token' in the new window and wait until the page refreshes.
Step 4:   Copy the 'Access Token' from the page and paste it here !!!.
